TEAM AXIS CODE OF CONDUCT FOR INSTRUCTORS

All Coaches/Instructors must respect the rights, dignity and worth of every human being and their ultimate right to self-determination. Specifically, coaches must treat everyone equitably and sensitively, within the context of their activity and ability, regardless of gender, ethnic origin, cultural background, sexual orientation, religion or political affiliation.

Code of Conduct for Instructors
a. I will arrive no later than 15minutes before the start of the assignment.
b. I will have to inform Team Axis Programme Coordinator no later than 24hrs beforehand if I am not feeling well, and/or not able to conduct the session. Failing which I will lose *1 session of coaching fees equivalent to that which I have missed (fees to be given to the last minute replacement coach/instructor).

1. I must respect the rights, dignity and worth of each and every person and treat each equally within the context of the sport.

2. I must place the well-being and safety of each player above all other considerations.

3. I must adhere to all guidelines laid down by governing bodies (NSAs, Sports SG) and Team Axis Pte Ltd.

4. I must develop an appropriate working relationship with the participants based on mutual trust and respect.

5. I must not exert undue influence to obtain personal benefit or reward.

6. I must encourage and guide participants to accept responsibility for their own behaviour and performance.

7. I must ensure that the activities I direct or advocate are appropriate for the age, maturity, experience and ability of participants.

8. I must co-operate fully with, Team Axis Sports Coordinator, teachers etc, in the best interests of the students/athletes.

9. I must always promote the positive aspects of the sport (e.g. fair play).

10. I must consistently display high standards of behaviour and appearance.

11. I must not use or tolerate inappropriate language.

12. I must not have any physical contact with my students under my charge.

- Instructors are not to physically handle any student, nor administer corporal punishment of any kind whatsoever.

- Any student not cooperating and misbehaving will be reported to the teacher-in-charge and the matter be referred to Team Axis Programme Coordinator.

- All Instructors must carry a whistle during coaching.

- When in doubt, always refer to Team Axis Programme coordinator or teacher in charge.

Dress Code for Instructors

1. All coaches are expected to enforce the school dress code.

2. Coaches are expected to dress appropriately as a coach and role model.

3. No slippers/sandals are to be worn in School Premises at all times.

4. Tattoos are strictly to be covered up.

5. No dyed hair is to be shown and must be covered up by a cap.

6. Earrings and/or facial jewelry are not to be worn.

7. Team Axis official Instructor uniform is to be worn when coaching

8. Smoking is strictly not allowed within 100 meters of the School Premises.

To be a qualified Team Axis instructor, the person must possess the following:

1. National Coaching Accreditation Programme (NCAP) Theory, and/or Technical Certification with VPS (Sports SG) or its equivalent.

2. Any other relevant certification required for the programme

3. Valid Standard First Aid, CPR & AED Certification

4. MOE IRS Registered (If coaching in any MOE Schools)

*Note* Any instructor found in breach of the above mentioned Dress Code & Code of Conduct may have part or whole of his/her coaching fee forfeited. Depending on the severity of the offense, Team Axis may report the incident to the regulating authorities.
